Welcome to Aggarwal Sweets And Restaurant

Now Available in Online

Agarwal Sweets was established in the year 1984. It was founded by Rohtas Kumar Aggarwal in 2005. He introduced North Indian Sweets for the first time in Erode. Consequently, he also started manufacturing delicious Bengali Sweets in 2008. This gave rise to the evolution of Sweets and it mainly concentrated on the development of Sweets industry and contributed greatly to the expansion and setup of Agarwal Sweets group in Erode.

Agarwal Sweets is today Erodes premier Indian Sweets manufacturer. They are the front runners in the manufacture of Sweets & Savories with the best hygiene standards. In fact Agarwal Sweets has the distinction of being the first to introduce Pure Ghee Sweets to our city. Today Agarwal Sweets has branched out greatly and its customers include corporate houses, Wedding halls, Educational Institutions and many more.
